Conversion

The speed in kilometers per hour is equal to the speed in miles per hour multiplied by 1.609344. Since one mile per hour is equal to 1.609344 kilometers per hour, that's the conversion ratio used in the formula.



Sonic Speed

Miles per hour (mph, m.p.h., MPH, or mi/h) is a British imperial and United States customary unit of speed expressing the number of miles travelled in one hour. It is used in the United Kingdom, the United States, and a number of smaller countries, most of which are UK or US territories, or have close historical ties with the UK or US. reference
